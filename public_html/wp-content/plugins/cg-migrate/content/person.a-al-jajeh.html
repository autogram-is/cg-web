As CFO, Al is responsible for managing the company’s financial health worldwide as well as supporting a wide range of strategic initiatives to help advance firm growth goals. Al’s 20 years of industry experience includes establishing and driving various corporate functions, such as finance, strategy, enterprise risk management, and procurement. Prior to joining Cumming Group, Al spent five-and-a-half years as Chief Financial & Strategy Officer for ASTAD Project Management in Doha, Qatar, and before that held executive-level positions with Weatherford International and Gannett/USA Today.
